## &nbsp; About Me

```text
10+ years building the infrastructure the world runs on.  Now teaching machines to reason on top of it.
```

I'm a **Senior Software Engineer at Red Hat** with deep roots in **Kubernetes, OpenShift, and large-scale SRE**.
Over the past decade I've designed platforms that kept distributed systems reliable under real-world pressure --
from bare-metal clusters to multi-cloud production fleets.

Today I'm channelling that operational depth into **AI Engineering** -- currently pursuing a
**Post Graduate programme in AI at IIT Jodhpur**, building production-grade **LLM and RAG systems**
that are grounded, observable, and cloud-native from day one.
